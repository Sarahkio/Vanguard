What are Movies/Shows?

Movies talk about different things and are creative. Movies have a clear beginning, middle and end. A movie may run about two to three hours.

TV show/series are those that are based on concepts that sell at the moment. TV shows are episodic and allow for multiple beginnings, middles and ends. Each TV script is part of a larger narrative, with multiple character and story arcs divided across a number of episodes and seasons. TV series does not have any time schedule.
